# A list of provocative questions and prompts that you can use to get to know someone better.
What is your biggest fear?
What is the most embarrassing thing you've ever done?
Who inspires you and why?
What past action are you not proud of?
What is a characteristic of yours that you struggle with?
If age and health and money were not issues, what would you do right now?
Describe a time where you did not fail gracefully.
Describe a time where didn't do the right thing.
What is something you're scared to tell the other person?
What is something you wish you did better?
